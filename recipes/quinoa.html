Ingredients

  • 1 cup Quinoa
  • 1 cup water or broth

Steps

  1. Rinse quinoa for 30 seconds
  2. Add 1 cup quinoa and 1 cup water to instant pot
  3. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes
  4. Natural release for 10 minutes
  5. Fluff with fork and serve warm
